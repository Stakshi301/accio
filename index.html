The Beagle Blog

TheBeagleis a breed of small scent hound, similar in appearance to the much larger foxhound. Thebeagle was developed primarily for hunting rabbit or hare, known as beagling. Possessing a great sense of smell and superior tracking instincts, the beagleis the primary breed used as a detection dog for prohibited agricultural imports and foodstuffs in quarantine around the world. Thebeagleis a popular pet due to its size and good temper.
